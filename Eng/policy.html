  • 1. I am working for quality,not time.
  • 2. When scheduling an appointment with me I need you to give me your first and second name and your telephone number.
  • 3. When scheduling an appointment I am kindly asking you to send me pictures or just ideas of the nail set you want at least one day before.
  • 4. You will be notified for your appointment one day before the actual appointment.

Thank you for the understanding!
