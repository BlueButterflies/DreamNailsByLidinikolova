Combi Manicure

Combi manicure is a very popular way of preparing the nail plate and cuticle area using e-file as well as traditional manicure tools to achieve a great looking manicure. This technic comes originally from Russia and nowadays it is combined with the technique of leveling the natural nail surface with the help of shellac ( gel polish ) base coat or with hard gel. In combi manicure + shellac we do “deep polish application” which visually makes the gel polish look like it grows out of your nails and also helps with having a good looking manicure for more time.
